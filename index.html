Workspace Lighting

Proper lighting reduces eye strain and improves focus during long work sessions. These picks emphasize adjustability, color temperature control, and flicker-free operation for comfortable extended use.

A desk lamp with adjustable color temperature (typically 2700K-6500K) allows you to switch between warm light for relaxation and cool daylight for focused work. Look for flicker-free models with memory function that remembers your last setting. BenQ, TaoTronics, and Phive make well-reviewed options.

  • Adjustable color temperature for different tasks
  • Dimming control for brightness levels
  • Flicker-free LED prevents eye fatigue
  • Flexible arm for positioning light precisely
